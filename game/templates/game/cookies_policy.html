2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used by website owners to make their websites work or to work more efficiently.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use essential cookies for the operation of our Site. These cookies are necessary to provide you with services available through our Site and to use some of its features. Additionally, we use analytics cookies, such as Google Analytics, to help us understand how our Site is used and to improve your experience. Without these cookies, certain functionalities of the Site would not be possible.

4. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Session Cookies: These cookies are essential for maintaining your game session. They store a unique session ID that allows us to keep track of your progress and provide a seamless gaming experience.
  • CSRF Protection Cookies: These cookies are used to protect your data by preventing Cross-Site Request Forgery attacks. They ensure that any data submission or form entry is secure and originates from our Site.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use Google Analytics to collect information about how visitors use our Site. This helps us analyze traffic, understand user behavior, and improve our Site. Google Analytics may use cookies to track your interactions with our Site. The data collected is subject to Google's [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

5. Your Choices Regarding Cookies

Since we only use essential cookies that are necessary for the functioning of our Site, these cannot be disabled without affecting the performance of the Site. However, you have the option to disable analytics cookies, such as those used by Google Analytics, through your browser settings or by using opt-out tools provided by Google. By using our Site, you agree to the use of these essential cookies.

6. Managing Cookies

You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, including those used by Google Analytics, but some parts of the Site may not function properly as a result. For more information on how to manage cookies in your browser settings, please visit your browser's help documentation. Additionally, you can manage your Google Analytics preferences through the [Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
